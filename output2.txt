From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia




Walmart has been the world's largest company by revenue since 2014.[1]
This list comprises the world's largest companies by consolidated revenue, according to the Fortune Global 500 2024 rankings and other sources.[2] American retail corporation Walmart has been the world's largest company by revenue since 2014.[1] The list is limited to the largest 50 companies, all of which have annual revenues exceeding US$130 billion. This list is incomplete, as not all companies disclose their information to the media or general public.[3] Out of 50 largest companies 23 are American, 17 Asian and 10 European.[2]


List[edit]
Information in the list relates to the most recent fiscal year (mostly FY 2023 or 2024).
